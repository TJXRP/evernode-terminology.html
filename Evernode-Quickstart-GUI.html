Evernode QuickStart

Generate your Evernode wallet instantly. Follow these detailed steps:

1. Click "Create Wallet" to generate a unique wallet address.

2. Save your wallet address and secret key securely; you'll need them later.

Send XRP to fund your wallet. Follow these steps:

1. Copy your new wallet address.

2. Paste it in the "Wallet Address" field and fund it with XRP.

To interact with EVR, add a trustline:

1. Click "Add Trustline" to allow EVR transactions.

2. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

Swap XRP for EVR:

1. Enter the amount of XRP you wish to convert.

2. Click "Convert to EVR" to complete the swap.

Enable Xahau wallet functionality:

1. Click "Activate Wallet" to unlock advanced features.

2. Wait for confirmation that your Xahau wallet is active.

Bridge your EVR to Xahau:

1. Confirm EVR balance and destination network.

2. Click "Bridge to Xahau" to initiate the transfer.

Deploy a WordPress site on Evernode:

1. Ensure EVR balance is sufficient for hosting.

2. Click "Launch Site" to deploy WordPress with one click.
